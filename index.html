Melbourne High School Railway Interest Group

Guest speakers, excursions, advocacy, and education for the students of Melbourne High School on public transport.

Learn More

Fostering Railway Enthusiasm For Over 70 Years

The Melbourne High School Railway Interest Group is the largest student-run organisation at the School. It was founded in 1955 during the active Club Scene at Melbourne High School, with Mr Newell acting as Teacher Liaison. The club was revived in 2021, and now takes an active role in the School community.
