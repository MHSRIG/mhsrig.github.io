About Us

The Railway Interest Group (RIG) brings together students interested in railways, transport, history and excursions across Victoria. The Melbourne High School Railway Club was founded in 1955, with 30 members under the watching eye of Mr Newell. This was part of the active Club Scene at Melbourne High School that developed in the 1930s and encouraged by a Friday period for club activities. In 2021, Rayyaan Khaleel revived the club as the Railway Interest Group. It has since grown to become the largest student-led club at Melbourne High School. Since its inception, RIG has hosted countless activities and excursions across Victoria, invited prominent guest speakers (e.g., Victorian Transport Minister Gabrielle Williams, transport YouTubers Taitset and Phillip Mallis etc.). We also have taken part in introducing new students into the school, and, importantly, been active in various local advocacy efforts such as the Yarra St Footpath and Route 58 Accessibility Campaigns.
